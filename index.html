Discover the Eastern Himalayas Like Never Before

Begin your journey in Maney Bhanjyang, a tranquil hamlet nestled in the lap of the Himalayas, and set out on the legendary Sandakphu-Phalut trek — a soul-stirring adventure through misty forests, alpine villages, and ridgeline trails that reveal awe-inspiring views of Kanchenjunga and even distant glimpses of Everest.
Follow the Singalila Ridge across timeless mountain hamlets like Chitrey, Meghma, Tonglu, Tumling, Gairibas, Kalapokhari, and finally reach the legendary peaks of Sandakphu and Phalut. Travel vintage Land Rovers, stay in cozy homestays, and witness Himalayan life in its purest form.

Your Gateway to the Singalila Ridge Experience .

We are passionate local experts dedicated to bringing you the most immersive Himalayan trekking experience through the pristine landscapes of West Bengal’s Darjeeling district.
Starting from the peaceful mountain village of Maney Bhanjyang, we guide you through the historic Sandakphu-Phalut trail, a high-altitude journey through a string of enchanting villages — Chitrey’s monastery, Meghma’s misty ridges, Tonglu and Tumling’s panoramic outlooks, Gairibas’ forested trails, Kalapokhari’s sacred lake, and finally the twin summits of Sandakphu and Phalut, where nature unveils its grandest spectacle.
